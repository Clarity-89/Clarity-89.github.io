Bio

I am a self-taught web-developer, living in Helsinki, Finland. Although I already have some working experience and a degree in Law, I realised that programming, and web development in particular, is something I would like to do. First as a hobby and then hopefully as a profession.

At the moment I am concentrating on improving my front-end skills and moving on to studying back-end development. At the meantime I am open to internship or job opportunities.

I am passionate about learning new things and applying the knowledge to build exciting stuff. Computers aside, I enjoy sports, listening to the music and reading books. I am also one of the content moderators/administrators on a music website got-djent.com.
